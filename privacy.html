Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This policy describes what information Skywalker's Surplus collects, how it is used, and how it is protected.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information in two ways: when you voluntarily submit it, and automatically through our analytics system.

What you submit — when you use the contact form, offer form, or AI chat:

  • Your name and email address
  • Messages and questions you submit
  • Offer amounts and any notes you include
  • Chat messages sent to Cosmo, the AI assistant

Analytics — this site uses Edge Analytics, a self-hosted, privacy-respecting analytics tool. No cookies or third-party tracking scripts are used. On each visit it collects:

  • Pages visited, time spent, and scroll depth
  • Approximate location (country, city, region) derived from your IP address by Cloudflare's edge network — your IP is not stored
  • Browser type, screen size, and language preference
  • Referrer URL and UTM campaign parameters if present
  • Conversion events: when an offer is submitted, a contact message is sent, or the AI chat is used

Analytics data is associated with an anonymous session ID that expires when you close your browser tab. It is not linked to your name, email, or any submitted information. If your browser has Do Not Track or Global Privacy Control enabled, analytics are skipped entirely.

2. How We Use Your Information

Information you submit is used solely to respond to your inquiry, evaluate your offer, or answer your question. We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties.

3. AI Chat (Cosmo)

This site includes an AI chat assistant named Cosmo. Messages you send to Cosmo are processed by our backend and may be sent to a large language model (LLM) to generate a response. Chat messages are not stored permanently and are not linked to your identity.

4. Data Retention

Contact and offer submissions are retained only as long as needed to complete the transaction or respond to your inquiry. You may request deletion of your data at any time via the contact form.

5. Security

This site is hosted on Cloudflare Pages and served over HTTPS. We take reasonable precautions to protect submitted information, but no transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Contact

Privacy questions or data requests: contact form

7. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated at any time. The date above reflects the most recent revision.
